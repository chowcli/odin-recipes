Oven-Baked Chicken Tacos

Description

For this dish, you’ll bake a hearty taco filling—featuring tender bites of chicken, fresh tomatoes, smoky guajillo sauce, and more—to serve inside soft flour tortillas. You’ll finish the tacos off with melty cheese and a dollop of creamy guacamole.

Ingredients

  • 10 oz Boneless Chicken Breast Pieces
  • 4 Flour Tortillas
  • 1 oz Sliced Pickled Jalapeño Pepper
  • 4 oz Grape Tomatoes
  • 2 oz Shredded Cheddar & Monterey Jack Cheese Blend
  • 1/4 cup Guacamole
  • 1/3 cup Guajillo Chile Pepper Sauce
  • 1 8-oz can Tomato Sauce
  • 1 Tbsp Mexican Spice Blend
  • 1 Single-Use Aluminum Tray

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F.
  2. Wash and dry the tomatoes.
  3. Pat the chicken dry with paper towels. Season with salt, pepper, and enough of the spice blend to coat (you may have extra).
  4. In the tray, combine the seasoned chicken, tomatoes, guajillo sauce, and tomato sauce. Season with salt and pepper; stir to thoroughly combine.
  5. Bake, uncovered, 19 to 21 minutes, or until slightly thickened and the chicken is cooked through.
  6. Meanwhile, wrap the tortillas in foil and place directly onto an oven rack; warm 7 to 9 minutes, or until heated through.
  7. Transfer to a work surface and carefully unwrap.
  8. Serve the baked tray with the warmed tortillas. Top with the cheese, guacamole, and as much of the pepper as you'd like, depending on how spicy you'd like the dish to be. Enjoy!
